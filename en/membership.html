Point Rules: For every 100 RMB spent, you earn 1 point. Unless otherwise noted, points are valid for two years from the date of acquisition and will expire automatically afterward.

Discount Policy: Membership discounts can be combined with other offers (such as early bird pricing), but not with coupon codes.

Upgrade Notes: Points accumulate across calendar years. If an order is canceled or refunded, the corresponding points will be deducted.
