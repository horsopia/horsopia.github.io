Who Are We?

HORSOPIA Internationale

Horsopia Internationale is an equestrian cultural platform originated in France and connects the world.

Founded in 2019 and officially registered in 2025 in Champigny-sur-Marne, France,

Horsopia is co-founded by a group of warm-hearted individuals brought together by their shared love of horses.The association is dedicated to promoting the European equestrian education system and fostering Sino-European equestrian exchange and industry development.

We focus on introducing equestrian courses, building career pathways, connecting international coaches with sport horse resources, and actively promoting cultural and artistic collaborations inspired by the equestrian world. Our aim is to create a platform that bridges China and France while reaching across Europe.

Over the past six years, the association has established in-depth partnerships with leading equestrian institutions across Europe, including in France and the Netherlands. We have organized youth training camps, adult programs, and private coaching for riders, continuously advancing both the localization and internationalization of equestrian education.

We believe that equestrianism is not merely a sport --
it is a cultural legacy and a profound connection of the soul.
