What is Horsopia Service?

We integrate Europe’s premium resources to deliver professional equestrian services including farriery, osteopathy, and sports rehabilitation.

Import of Elite French Coaches: We bring high-level coaches certified by the FFE (French Equestrian Federation) to China, where they serve as resident trainers at equestrian clubs or lead seasonal intensive training programs.

Import of Quality Sport Horses: We assist with trial riding, horse selection, and international transport. Several elite competition horses have already been successfully brought into China for use by riders and clubs.

Advanced Training & Rider Development Programs: We offer services including French rider registration, Galop certification preparation, specialized equestrian courses, and access to high-level international competitions for observation and learning.

Professional Equestrian Interpreters: Our interpreters provide specialized translation services for equestrian competitions, instructional settings, and industry-related business exchanges.

Our Service Includes:

Collaborating with Olympic riders by sourcing premium horses

Sharing resources with top foreign stables and bringing innovative training ideas

Hosting international coaches in China and supporting local trainers to study abroad

Building a global network with customized solutions and a dedicated team

Managing horse imports with full veterinary checks and safe transport

Ensuring new horses thrive and perform excellently in Chinese arenas
