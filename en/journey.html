What is Horsopia Journey?

Since 2019, we have established deep collaborations with renowned equestrian institutions in China and Europe. We offer year-round training camps and study tours tailored to all age groups and skill levels.

We also curate boutique horseback travel and immersive cultural tours, with routes spanning across France, the Netherlands, Germany, Spain, Portugal, and more countries under development.

We organize access to top-level equestrian competitions, including VIP seating, fine cuisine, and wine tastings — offering a fully immersive experience of the equestrian world.
